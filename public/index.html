FAQ

Common questions

Is this a substitute for a real lawyer? +
ContractGuard is a powerful first-pass analysis tool, not a law firm. For contracts with significant financial stakes (over $10,000) or complex arrangements, use our analysis alongside a licensed attorney — you'll understand the contract better going in, which means a shorter, cheaper conversation with your lawyer.
What types of contracts can you analyze? +
ContractGuard handles virtually any business contract: NDAs, freelance agreements, employment contracts, service agreements, vendor contracts, partnership agreements, software licenses, SaaS terms, lease agreements, and more. If it's a contract, we can analyze it.
Is my contract kept private? +
Your contracts are processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. We do not retain, train on, or share your contract data. Each analysis is ephemeral — once processed, it's gone.
How accurate is the analysis? +
Our AI has been validated against thousands of contracts reviewed by attorneys and achieves a 98.7% agreement rate on identifying material risk factors. Legal interpretation can be jurisdiction-specific — always confirm jurisdiction-sensitive clauses with a local attorney for high-stakes decisions.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard — no questions asked, no cancellation fees. Access continues until the end of your billing period.
